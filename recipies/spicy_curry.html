Wash and dice all vegetables. Chop onion, mince garlic and ginger, cube bell peppers, and slice mushrooms. Drain and rinse chickpeas if using canned.

Start cooking basmati rice according to package instructions. Add a pinch of salt and a bay leaf for extra flavor. This will serve as the base for your curry bowl.

Heat coconut oil in a large pan over medium heat. Add cumin seeds until they sizzle, then add onions. Cook until translucent, then add garlic, ginger, and green chilies.

4

Add curry powder, turmeric, coriander, and garam masala. Cook for 1 minute until fragrant. Add diced tomatoes and cook until they break down and become saucy.

5

Add bell peppers, mushrooms, and chickpeas. Pour in coconut milk and add salt. Bring to a gentle boil, then reduce heat and simmer until vegetables are tender.

‚è∞ 8 minutes
6

Add Greens and Final Seasonings

Stir in spinach leaves and let them wilt. Taste and adjust salt, add lime juice for brightness, and sprinkle in fresh cilantro. Let flavors meld together.

‚è∞ 5 minutes
7

Serve and Garnish

Serve the spicy curry over fluffy basmati rice. Garnish with fresh cilantro, a dollop of yogurt, and sliced red chilies. Serve with naan or flatbread if desired.

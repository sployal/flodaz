Davie Mwangi

Co-Founder & CEO

With over 8 years of experience in tech entrepreneurship and a lifelong passion for culinary arts, Davie brings together the perfect blend of innovation and tradition. His vision for democratizing recipe sharing stems from his childhood memories of family gatherings centered around his grandmother's kitchen.

Davie holds a degree in Computer Science and has previously founded two successful startups. When he's not coding or strategizing, you'll find him experimenting with fusion cuisines and hosting dinner parties for friends and family.
